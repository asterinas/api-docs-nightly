bigtcp is a crate that wraps [smoltcp].

[smoltcp] is designed for embedded systems where the number of sockets is always small. It turns out that such a design cannot satisfy the need to implement the network stack of a general-purpose OS kernel, in terms of flexibility and efficiency.

The short-term goal of bigtcp is to reuse the powerful TCP implementation of smoltcp, while reimplementing Ethernet and IP protocols to increase the flexibility and performance of packet dispatching.
